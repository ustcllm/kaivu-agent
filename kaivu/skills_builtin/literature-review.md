---
name: literature-review
description: Structured guidance for evidence-driven literature review.
when_to_use: Use when the task requires primary-source literature synthesis, citation hygiene, and uncertainty tracking.
allowed_tools: pubmed_search, arxiv_search, crossref_search, resolve_citation, search_memory, record_observation
---
Prioritize primary sources and systematic reviews over blogs or summaries.
Separate established findings from open questions.
When sources disagree, say so explicitly.
Track representative citations and unresolved evidence gaps.
